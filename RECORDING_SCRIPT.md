# Enablement Onboarding — Recording Script
**Duration**: 2-3 minutes
**Format**: Screen recording (Loom, QuickTime, or similar)
**Audience**: Anyone you send this to — no technical background assumed

---

## Before you record

- Have claude.ai open in your browser (logged into a Pro/Team/Enterprise account)
- Have the `enablement-coach.md` file open in another tab or ready to copy
- Clean up your browser — close other tabs, hide bookmarks bar if cluttered

---

## Script

### Opening (10 seconds)

*Show your face or just the screen — your call.*

"I'm going to show you how to set up your own AI coaching workspace. It takes about 3 minutes. After that, the coach guides you through everything."

### Step 1: Create a project (30 seconds)

*Screen: claude.ai, left sidebar visible.*

"Go to claude.ai. On the left, click Projects, then Create Project."

*Click through it live.*

"Name it whatever you want. I'll call mine 'My AI Toolkit'."

### Step 2: Add the coaching instructions (45 seconds)

*Screen: inside the new project, Project Instructions field visible.*

"Now find Project Instructions — it's this text box at the top. This is where you tell Claude how to behave in this project."

*Switch to the enablement-coach.md file. Select all. Copy.*

"I'm going to paste in the coaching instructions. You'll get this file from me — just select everything, copy it, and paste it here."

*Paste into Project Instructions.*

"That's it for setup."

### Step 3: First conversation (45 seconds)

*Screen: start a new chat inside the project.*

"Now start a new chat and just say hello."

*Type "hello" and send.*

*Wait for the coach's response. Let it load fully.*

"The coach introduces itself and starts asking about your work. It'll ask 3 to 5 questions, then build a personalized plan for you. Each session is about 20 to 40 minutes, and you go at your own pace."

### Close (15 seconds)

"That's it. The coach handles everything from here. If you have questions, just ask it — that's what it's for."

*End recording.*

---

## After recording

- Trim dead air at the start and end
- Upload to Loom (or your preferred tool)
- Share the link along with the enablement-coach.md file

When you send it, include something like:

> Here's a 2-minute video showing how to set up your AI coaching workspace. You'll also need this file — paste it into the Project Instructions like I show in the video. After that, just say hello and the coach takes it from there.
